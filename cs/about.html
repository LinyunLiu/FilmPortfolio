Who am I ? ✐

I have just received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with a Concentration in Media & Communication: Film Studies at Trinity Western University in Canada.

I love all fields of Computer Science and I have been motivated to try them all. As a huge nerd, I love to learn and making meaningful projects, it brings me tremendous joys to study and understand new things especially in the world of I.T.. I believe my greatest strength is determination of doing what I feel passionate about, such as software engineering, full stack developments, developing algorithms, etc. I have also been studying and practicing fine arts since kindergarten. With the artistic and emotional part of me, I cannot control myself to make websites and software as beautiful as possible. Problem Solving skill is what I often feel proud of, it is what I am particularly great at doing, I convince myself that there is always a solution to the problem, and I will be committed to fulfill that promise with my perfectionist mind, and I always did. And being a perfectionist, I have a habit to write code that is highly organized, and bugs free as possible as I can. I consider myself an author of a piece of code instead of a programmer.

I am in general very motivated to contribute to the world of technology with my skills, patience and creativity. I am always eager to find out what more I am capable of creating, innovating with the knowledge that I have been dedicating to learn. As a young individual, I also hope I can explore my potentials and possibilities to acquire more valuable experiences, to challenge myself, as well as making contributions to the growth of technologies.
